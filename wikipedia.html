The Design process




My goal here was to redesign the main pages from Wikipedia.
So I've focused on Home, Portal Home and Article pages with its main interactions.

01

Analysis

I first started looking what could be improved? What is already good? The visual could be improved, but what else? So I started pinpointing some stuff that I think could be improved.

02

Planning

I've talked with some people and gotten some information about Wikipedia. I asked them about their experience while using the website. And from their reviews and experience I started geathering some rough ideas how new UI will look.


After the examination, I've found that people don't trust Wikipedia by any means. So I felt that Its experience ought to be improved to make it feel more realiable.

The design that I wanted to make should be simple and visually engaging, yet reliable. With this in mind, I've set an expression to go about as a Design Principle to guide my idea.

Information Architecture
